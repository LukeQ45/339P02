Design Story
Reviews

French-born Christophe Delcourt graduated from Paris’ Cours Florent in 1988 and studied at the Theatre Ecole du Passage in 1989. Committed to designing furniture and objects, Delcourt opened his first studio in the Marais area of Paris in 1998. Since then, he has designed for restaurants, hotels and boutiques and has exhibited furniture and lamps at Joyce Gallery in Paris. Over the past decade, his Paris-based firm has produced some of the most coveted and influential furniture of the new millennium, including the Scoop, Rive Droite and limited-edition, ecoconscious Legend lines for Roche Bobois. Delcourt’s designs for the AVENUE ROAD editions include a solid wood furniture collection, a sofa system and lighting. AVENUE ROAD carries a curated assortment of Christophe Delcourt pieces.
