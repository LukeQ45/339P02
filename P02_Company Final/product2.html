Inspired by architectural marvels like Le Corbusier’s chapel in Ronchamp, the seating piec-es in the Echos family by the Delcourt Collection are intended to last a lifetime and pay tribute to a natural asceticism. With the EKO Meridienne, an expressive and sculptural design features tensed lines and curved shapes meant to interlock and interact with each other in a wide variety of configurations. The generously proportioned upholstery, available in customizable fabric and with an optional ottoman, evoke the soft peacefulness of elegant poetry.
